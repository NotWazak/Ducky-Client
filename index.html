About Ducky Client

Ducky Client is a free to use PvP oriented modpack for Minecraft 1.8.9. Ducky Client is on the bleeding edge of user experience and aesthetics in regards to Minecraft designs. Ducky Client is packed with a sleek and modern launcher for Mac, Linux and windows respectively. Ducky Client includes real and effective frame and system resource usage optimizations and many popular mods.

The World's Most Customizable PvP Client

With color themes that can be edited and loaded by the user, Ducky Client can be completely transformed visually!

Global Cosmetics

We have a fast, asynchronous, global and always online cosmetics system. Ducky Client has a large selection of cosmetics including: Cloaks, Wings and Bandanas! We also do paid capes giveaways and you can get free cosmetics! Cosmetics can be purchased at our store.

Modular Overlay Menu

Ducky Client operates off of a single menu. This menu can be opened anywhere in the client. The menu contains elements that can be opened from the task bar and can be moved by grabbing them!

HUD Position Screen

Allows the user to locate and resize hud modules with precision and accuracy.

Launcher

Our launcher supports Mac, Linux and Windows respectively. With Ducky Launcher you can log into Mojang and Microsoft accounts to launch the game quickly and get playing!
